# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 2, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Anne Marie Charrett ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and pay-per-article service.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address, name (when you create a free account)
- **Payment Information:** Processed securely by Stripe (we do not store credit card details)
- **Purchase History:** Records of articles you have purchased

### Information Automatically Collected
- **Usage Data:** Pages viewed, articles purchased, access timestamps
- **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, device information
- **Cookies:** Essential cookies for authentication and functionality

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Process article purchases and provide access to purchased content
- Manage your account and authenticate your access
- Send purchase receipts and access confirmations
- Improve our service and user experience
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

### Third-Party Services
We share data with the following service providers:

**Stripe (Payment Processing)**
- Purpose: Process payments securely
- Data Shared: Email, payment information
- Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

**Ghost CMS (Content Management)**
- Purpose: Manage member accounts and content access
- Data Shared: Email, account status
- Self-hosted on our infrastructure

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal process
- Protect our rights or property
- Prevent fraud or abuse

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

### GDPR Rights (EU/UK Users)
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate information
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Objection:** Object to processing of your data
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time
