# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** November 2, 2025

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide and improve our service.

## Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `ghost-members-ssr` | Authentication session | Session | Essential |
| `ghost.members-theme-session` | Theme access control | Session | Essential |

**Why we use them:**

- Keep you logged in to your account
- Remember your purchase status for content access
- Secure your session and prevent fraud

### Analytics Cookies (Optional - if implemented)

**We currently do NOT use analytics cookies.** If we add them in the future, we will:

- Update this policy

**Potential future analytics:**

## How to Manage Cookies

### Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

**Chrome:**

1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
2. Choose your preferred option

**Firefox:**

1. Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Customize settings as needed

**Safari:**

1. Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
2. Select your preference

**Edge:**

1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions
2. Manage cookie settings

### Effect of Disabling Cookies

**If you disable essential cookies:**

- You cannot log in to your account
- You cannot access purchased articles
- The service will not function properly

**If you disable analytics cookies (when implemented):**

- The website will still work normally
- We won't collect usage statistics

## Cookie Consent (GDPR/PECR Compliance)

### For EU/UK Users

Under GDPR and PECR, we:

- Only use essential cookies by default
- Will request consent before setting non-essential cookies
- Allow you to withdraw consent at any time
- Provide clear information about cookie purposes

### Consent Mechanism

Currently, since we only use essential cookies (exempt from consent requirements), no consent banner is shown.

**If we add analytics cookies in the future**, you will see a cookie banner allowing you to:

- Accept all cookies
- Accept only essential cookies
- Customize your preferences

## Local Storage and Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use:

### Local Storage

- **Purpose:** Cache content for faster loading
- **Data stored:** Access tokens (short-lived, 15 minutes)
- **Control:** Can be cleared via browser settings

### Session Storage

- **Purpose:** Temporary data during your browsing session
- **Data stored:** Purchase confirmation messages
- **Duration:** Cleared when you close the browser
