# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** November 2, 2025

We use cookies to make the site work. Here's what you need to know.

## What Are Cookies?

Small text files stored on your device that help us keep you logged in and deliver content you've paid for.

## What We Use

**Essential Cookies** (required for the site to work):

- **Authentication** - Keeps you logged in
- **Access control** - Remembers what content you can access
- **Security** - Protects your account

These cookies are necessary. You can't disable them without breaking the site.

**Analytics Cookies**:

- **Google Analytics** - Tracks which pages you visit, how long you read, and what's popular
- **Google Tag Manager** - Manages tracking scripts

You can opt out of analytics:
- Use browser settings (see below)
- Install [Google Analytics Opt-out extension](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

## Third-Party Cookies

**Stripe** - Sets cookies when you make a payment for fraud prevention and security. See [Stripe's cookie policy](https://stripe.com/cookies-policy/legal).

**Senja** - May set cookies for testimonial widgets. See [Senja's privacy policy](https://senja.io/privacy).

**Google** - Sets cookies for analytics tracking. See [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them means you won't be able to log in or access paid content.

**Browser Settings:**

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy
- **Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions
